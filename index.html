DUCH DYNIL

Mr. Duch Dynil holds a Master's Degree of EEAP specialty Management of Automated Systems from Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1, France and another Master's Degree of Mobile Technology from Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC). He is specialized in Natural Language Processing, Computer Sciences applied to Industrial Automation and Electrical Engineering, and Sensors Network for Safety and Energy Saving. He is also an outstanding iOS developer and Web developer with Django. KHEM PUTHEA

Mr. Khem Puthea teaches Web Design subject at NIPTICT where students will learn to build a website or web application with HTML (Hyper Text Markup Language) and CSS. SAM BOUEN

Mr. Hean Samboeun teaches Soft Skill Development for Engineering subject at NIPTICT where students will learn to understand the life principle, problem-solving, learning to learn, knowing oneself, leadership, interculturality, and empathy. He holds a Master's Degree in Information Technology in Artificial Intelligence System from Taganrog Institute of Technology, Russian Federation. He was also a researcher in AI System and Data Security during and after his studies. He has been working as an Instructor of Computer Science since 2006 and became the Director of e-Learning Center from 2014 to 2018. Currently, he is serving as a Vice-President of NIPTICT in charge of Academic Affairs. Mr. Samboeun believes that many businesses are now moving into digitalized platforms. Therefore, ICT/STEM majors are the key success for almost all sectors for now and in the near future.
